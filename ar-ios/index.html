Augmented Reality Examlpes

This page holds examples of various objects that can be rendered/viewed on your mobile device to enhance your customers experiences with your brand and products.

Click on an item to view it in real space and time. The objects can be moved, scaled, and rotated.

iOS: You can click on the item to view it natiively on your iphone for the best experience. You can select the "Object" element to take it out the real world space and view the 3D object in a controlled space.

Android: Users can scan the image markers to view the 3D model on the web or accomponying physical product.
