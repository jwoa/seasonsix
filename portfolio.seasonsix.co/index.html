Project OASIS

Web Application

A lot of people, primarily minorities, live in places known as food deserts. and swamps. Due to this they have terrible diets and poor health but there is not much that they can do about it since the bulk of the food in their area are not good for them and those that aren’t bought as much.

The primary purpose of this project is to show people wether or not they live in food swamps and if they do, what they can do to try and get healthier foods in their communities, how they can contact their local representatives to make a change and any other options. There are websites like this that exist but their design and functionality are just plain terrible. They live strictly to display data and they are not user friendly.They were not made for people to use but more so made for researchers and data analysis.

OASIS is going to make this user friendly for everyone.

homies

iOS Application

I wanted to take a shot at designing a mobile app, but one that would be needed. Something that I could relate to and something that I know wouldn’t just sit in the App Store collecting dust. So I thought about something that could help me and my homies.

When you live with other people it’s important for you all to be on the same page. Although the App Store has numerous apps that help with living with someone there’s not one cohesive fun app to make everything simple.

Living with two of my best friends I realized how “clutch” it would be to have something that can take of everything and simplify our complications. So I decided to do something about it.

D.I.Y Religion

Glitch Experiment

One of my best friends, Jamal (Mally), once spoke to me about how we all follow the same path and engage in certain social experiences depending on where we live.

This whole idea of following the same path and viewing things differently led my mind to religion. A construct with numerous similarities across all variations and a shared goal of peace, love, and respect for others as well as God. Yet it is the cause of millions of deaths and wars. A path with the same destination but depending on how you decide to travel your experience is either slightly or completely different.

This project shows what happens when an individual puts his perspective on religion.

Glitch Experiment

However, I had not done any real research into the matter, I just thought that eating fruits and cutting out the junk would work. After looking into where my fruits were coming from and what was actually in most of them I decided to make a statement with it.
