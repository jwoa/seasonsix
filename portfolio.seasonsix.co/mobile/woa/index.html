Hey there, I’m Jason Agyekum and I’m a designer + developer who hopes to make cool and useful things for people. Although I started with only making websites I found that there were more possibilities to create greater things with interaction design and I’ve changed my path ever since.

The great thing about interaction design is that it can applied to different mediums like websites, products, services etc. The possibilities are endless and I would love to further develop my skills so I can take them on.

homies


When you live with other people it’s important for you all to be on the same page. Although the App Store has numerous apps that help with living with someone there’s not one cohesive fun app to make everything simple.

Living with two of my best friends I realized how “clutch” it would be to have something that can take of everything and simplify our complications. So I decided to do something about it.

D.I.Y Religion

One of my best friends, Jamal (Mally), once spoke to me about how we all follow the same path and engage in certain social experiences depending on where we live. But, because of perspective we all experience things differently. This whole idea of following the same path and viewing things differently led my mind to religion. A construct with numerous similarities across all variations and a shared goal of peace, love, and respect for others as well as God. Yet it is the cause of millions of deaths and wars. This projects looks into it.
